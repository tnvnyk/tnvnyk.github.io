Graph Recovery | IISc Internship

Focus: Developed a graph recovery algorithm in Python by combining sparse Bayesian learning with DAG constraints.

Outcome: Under low-sample regimes, achieved a 16.7% reduction in SHD over the strongest baseline and 58–71% lower SHD compared to other baseline methods.
